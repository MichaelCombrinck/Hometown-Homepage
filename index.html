Top three activities to do at Torshov

The Big Hole

A place where the first diamond was found South-Africa, to see all the minearals and history.

Explore the William Humphreys Art Gallery

The William Humphreys Art Gallery is a cultural gem in Kimberley, housing an extensive collection of South African and international artworks. Named after its benefactor, the gallery aims to promote visual arts and provide educational opportunities for the community.

Discover the Magersfontein Battlefield

The Magersfontein Battlefield, located just outside Kimberley, is a significant historical site from the Second Anglo-Boer War. The battle, fought in December 1899, was a major conflict between the British forces and Boer troops.
